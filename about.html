Our Story

Dedicated to Government Excellence

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between innovative technology and government operations, 638 Government Services has grown to become a trusted partner for public sector organizations across the United States.

Our journey began with a simple observation: government agencies face unique challenges that require specialized solutions. While private sector technology was advancing rapidly, government entities struggled to adopt these innovations while maintaining security, compliance, and public trust.

We built our company around the principle that government technology should be secure, reliable, and citizen-centric. Every solution we deliver is designed with these core values in mind.
